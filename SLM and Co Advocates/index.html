THE FIRM

The firm, “Sempasa, Luwaga, Muwemba & Company Advocates” (SLM) was established in 2020 by the three founding partners, Dr. Samson L. Sempasa, Mrs. Solome Mayinja Luwaga, and Mrs. Evelyn Nkalubo-Muwemba. All three are well seasoned legal counselors with extensive experience as senior Advocates and as corporate and transactional attorneys. SLM is a client-focused full-service law firm that is committed to serving the needs of its clients. We understand that the legal climate is constantly evolving, but each client’s need is usually special and unique in its own way. We therefore endeavor to develop tailor-made strategies and approaches to address the unique requirements of each client. SLM is comprised of tried and tested Senior Partners who are supported by a team of exceptional, dedicated and hardworking Associates. All are committed to providing quality legal services through all facets of legal representation as required. Because of our extensive legal representation experience, SLM team is able to efficiently and effectively handle both court cases and transactional matters

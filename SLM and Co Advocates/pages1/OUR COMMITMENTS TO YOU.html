OUR COMMITMENTS TO YOU

  • To provide you with competent and effective representation
  • To advise you on course of action based on your rights and obligations
  • To zealously pursue your representation within the bounds of law
  • To promptly inform you of decisions and circumstances as needed to secure your informed consent
  • To reasonably consult you about means to accomplish your objectives
  • To keep you reasonably informed about the status of your matter
  • To promptly comply with your reasonable requests for information
  • To explain a matter as reason necessary to permit you to make informed decisions
  • To apply ourselves with knowledge, skills, thoroughness and preparation reasonably necessary for undertaking your representation

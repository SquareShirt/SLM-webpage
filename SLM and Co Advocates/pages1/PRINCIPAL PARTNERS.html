EVELYN NKALUBO-MUWEMBA

Ms. Nkalubo-Muwemba is a legal and business management multi-skilled professional with cross functional expertise in governance and strategy execution, people and financial management and business processes improvement spanning over two and a half decades. Evelyn is currently the managing partner of SLM. She was the Director Legal and Secretary to the Insurance Regulatory Authority of Uganda (IRA) from 1997 to 2020. She also acted as the Commissioner for Insurance (CEO of the Insurance Regulatory Authority) from 2008 to 2011. Evelyn also worked with the Economic Policy Research Centre as a Programme Administrator and with the Uganda Manufacturer’s Association in various capacities i.e. Director Planning & Management, Programme Administrator, Administrative & Finance Officer etc.

she also served at the Uganda Commercial Bank in the legal department (under the Development Finance Group) as a Legal Officer/ Monitoring and Loan Processor. She started her career as a State Attorney with the Attorney General Chambers and Assistant Secretary to the Uganda Law Council while at the Ministry of Justice. She has served and continues to serve as a member on the Boards of corporations and Non-Governmental organisations. Evelyn holds a Master of Business Administration (MBA), University of Stirling UK, a Bachelor of Laws Degree from Makerere University and a Diploma in Legal Practice (Dip. LP) from the Law Development Centre-Kampala.

She is an Advocate of the High Court of Uganda and all courts of judicature in Uganda and has practiced law since 1985. She is a Chartered Insurer (ACII), UK; an Associate of the Insurance Institute of Uganda (AIIU); and an Associate of the Chartered Institute of Arbitrators (ACIArb) – UK. She is a member of the East Africa Law Society, the Uganda Law Society and the Uganda Association of Women Lawyers (FIDA-Uganda)

SAMSON L.SEMPASA

Dr. Sempasa has over 30 years of experience as a law practitioner and legal consultant and 28 years of experience mediating over 1200 cases involving a wide range of subject matter. He is a licensed Advocate of the High Court and all courts of judicature in Uganda and is also licensed to practice before the Supreme Court of the United States of America, the 5th Circuit Court of Appeal of United States, and the Supreme Court of the State of Louisiana. He is a member of Uganda Law Society, Louisiana State Bar Association, California State Bar Association, and East African Law Society. Dr. Sempasa is also an accredited mediator/Arbitrator of the Center for Arbitration and Dispute Resolution (CADER), the American Arbitration Association (AAA), Center for Effective Dispute Resolution (CEDR) and the International Mediation Institute. He is recognized as one of Uganda’s foremost commercial mediator/arbitrator and he writes and speaks frequently on issues related to ADR and effective resolution of disputes

He is an experienced international development practitioner with extensive field experience in justice sector reforms. He directed justice sector reforms, ADR trainings and system designs in Uganda, Kenya, Rwanda, Tanzania, South Sudan, Ethiopia, Nigeria, Liberia, Lesotho, Afghanistan and United States. He is a founder and the first Executive Director of CADER. He has also delivered Mediation and Conflict Resolution trainings both domestically and internationally.

Dr. Sempasa holds a doctorate of Juridical Sciences (SJD) from Tulane University Law School in New Orleans, Louisiana, USA; a Master of International and Comparative Laws (LL.M-MCL) [With Distinction] from Tulane University Law School, New Orleans Louisiana, USA; a Bachelor of Laws LL.B [Hons.] from Makerere University; and a Diploma in Legal Practice (Dip.LP) from the Law Development Center–Kampala, Uganda. He is a member of the Uganda Law Society, East African Law Society.

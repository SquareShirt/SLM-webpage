BANKRUPTCY, INSOLVENCY & RESTRUCTURING

Here, SLM team represents a wide range of clients to deliver bankruptcy-restructuring and insolvency management services. We represent interests of debtors, public and private corporations, liquidators, examiners, officeholders and secured and unsecured creditors to offer a wide range of services that include: individual or business rescue mechanisms; bankruptcy or insolvency representation; representation at investigations; loan restructuring; debtor-in-possession options; managing lender liability disputes; assistance with sale and purchase of distressed assets; managing sale and purchase of debts, and more.

We assist debtors and businesses to manage distress and help with business restructuring and rescue. We also assist clients to pursue rehabilitation processes for businesses that are financially distressed. We advise on debt restructure, cutting expenses, laying-off staff, and selective sale of assets in order to continue as a going concern.

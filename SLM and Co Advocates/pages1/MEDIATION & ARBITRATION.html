MEDIATION & ARBITRATION

There has been a remarkable responsiveness to the use of Alternative Dispute Resolution processes (ADR) throughout the entire dispute resolution landscape the world over. Courts, government agencies and disputants have all embraced mediation, arbitration and conciliation as more efficient and costeffective ways by which to resolve many kinds of disputes at all levels of conflicts—from beginning to end. SLM’s well-experienced, accredited and court approved Mediators and Arbitrators strive to facilitate expeditious resolution of clients’ disputes while maintaining and mending their relationships. Although the court system is a good and fair way of dealing with civil disputes, it may not always be the best method by which to resolve particular disputes.

In all instances, SLM’s skilled Mediators/Arbitrators will employ ADR approaches, especially mediation, to assist in the resolution of matters in a cost-effective manner that fosters long term relationships. We provide strategic dispute analysis and practical advice based on realistic assessments of outcomes and potential costs, and assist our clients to make informed decisions that are consistent with their business objectives. SLM has represented clients in some of the biggest commercial disputes and in landmark cases in Uganda and internationally. Our well experienced and internationally accredited Arbitrators and Mediators will successfully represent you or act as your appointed neutral in domestic or foreign disputes.
